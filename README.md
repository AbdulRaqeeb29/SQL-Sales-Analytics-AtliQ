# Atliq Hardware Sales Analysis (SQL Project)

## Project Overview

This project analyzes the sales performance of Atliq Hardware, a computer hardware company operating across multiple international markets. The objective was to explore the company’s transactional sales data and generate business insights related to market performance, product contribution, and customer revenue distribution.

Using SQL with MySQL, I wrote analytical queries to explore the dataset and answer real-world business questions that stakeholders typically ask in sales analytics.

The dataset contains 1.4+ million sales records across multiple markets, customers, and products.

## Business Questions Solved

**The analysis focused on answering key business questions such as:**

- Which markets generate the highest revenue?

- Which products drive the company’s sales?

- Which customers contribute the most revenue?

- How has the company’s revenue grown over time?

- What is the year-over-year sales growth trend?


## SQL Concepts Used

**During the analysis, several SQL techniques were applied:**

- Joins to combine multiple tables

- Aggregations for revenue calculations

- Window Functions for year-over-year analysis

- Subqueries for intermediate calculations

- CASE statements for segmentation and categorization

- Common Table Expressions (CTE) for improved query readability

- A total of 13 analytical SQL queries were written to explore the dataset and generate insights.

## Key Insights from the Analysis

- India and USA were identified as the top revenue-generating markets.

- The company experienced strong year-over-year revenue growth, increasing significantly from $73M in 2018 to $3.56B in 2021.

- Products such as AQ BZ Compact and AQ Smash 1 emerged as major contributors to total revenue.

- Large enterprise customers including Amazon and Atliq e Store were identified as key revenue drivers.
